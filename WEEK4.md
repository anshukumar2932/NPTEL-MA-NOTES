# 🧠 PART 1: DEMAND & PRICE RELATIONSHIP

---

### ❓ Q1. What happens to demand when price decreases?
👉 Demand **increases** (Law of Demand)

---

### ❓ Q2. What happens when price increases?
👉 Demand **decreases**

---

### ❓ Q3. Why does demand increase when price falls?
👉 
- More affordable  
- More customers willing to buy  

---

### ❓ Q4. Is this always true?
👉 In most cases (exceptions exist but ignore for exam)

---

# 🔴 PART 2: FORECASTING (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q5. What is forecasting?
👉 Using past data to **predict future values**

---

### ❓ Q6. Why is forecasting important?
👉 
- Planning production  
- Inventory management  
- Budgeting  

---

### ❓ Q7. Forecasting uses?
👉 Historical data  

---

### ❓ Q8. What is judgmental forecasting?
👉 Used when:
- No past data  
- New product  
- Uncertain conditions  

---

### ❓ Q9. Time series forecasting is used for?
👉 Analyzing past data over time  

---

### ❓ Q10. Time series means?
👉 Data collected over time  

---

# 🔴 PART 3: FORECAST ERROR (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q11. What is forecast error?
👉 Difference between actual and predicted value  

---

### ❓ Q12. Formula:

👉 Error = Actual – Forecast  

---

### ❓ Q13. Example:

👉 Actual = 125  
👉 Forecast = 110  

👉 Error = 125 – 110 = **+15**

---

### ❓ Q14. What does positive error mean?
👉 Underestimation  

---

### ❓ Q15. What does negative error mean?
👉 Overestimation  

---

# 🔴 PART 4: RMSE (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

### ❓ Q16. What is RMSE?
👉 Root Mean Square Error  

---

### ❓ Q17. What does RMSE measure?
👉 How close data points are to regression line  

---

### ❓ Q18. Interpretation:
👉 Lower RMSE → better model  

---

### ❓ Q19. Why RMSE is important?
👉 Measures model accuracy

# 🔴 PART 6: PRODUCT LIFE CYCLE (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q24. What is Product Life Cycle (PLC)?

---

### ❓ Q25. Stages of PLC?

👉

2. Growth  
3. Maturity  
4. Decline  

---

### ❓ Q26. Features of Introduction stage?

👉 
- Demand must be created  
- Slow sales  
- High costs

### ❓ Q27. Is profit high in introduction stage?
👉 ❌ No  

---

### ❓ Q28. Why profit is low initially?
👉 High marketing & production cost
