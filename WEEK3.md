# 🧠 PART 1: SEGMENTATION (CORE THEORY 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q1. What is market segmentation?
👉 Dividing a large market into **smaller, homogeneous groups** of customers.

---

### 💡 Key Idea:
👉 Similar within group  
👉 Different across groups  

---

### ❓ Q2. Why segmentation is important?
👉 
- Better targeting  
- Efficient marketing  
- Higher customer satisfaction  

---

### ❓ Q3. Is segmentation same as positioning?
👉 ❌ No  

👉 Segmentation = divide market  
👉 Positioning = create brand image  

---

---

### ❓ Q18. Correct sequence of marketing strategy?

👉 
1. Segmentation  
2. Targeting  
3. Positioning

1. Position services in such a way that resonates with the selected target market or markets.
2. Find highly profitable segments and target them.
3. Divide market into potential group segments.
👉 Answer: **3 → 2 → 1 (in question format)**
