# 🧠 PART 1: SEGMENTATION (CORE THEORY 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q1. What is market segmentation?
👉 Dividing a large market into **smaller, homogeneous groups** of customers.

---

### 💡 Key Idea:
👉 Similar within group  
👉 Different across groups  

---

### ❓ Q2. Why segmentation is important?
👉 
- Better targeting  
- Efficient marketing  
- Higher customer satisfaction  

---

### ❓ Q3. Is segmentation same as positioning?
👉 ❌ No  

👉 Segmentation = divide market  
👉 Positioning = create brand image  

---

# 🔴 PART 2: TYPES OF SEGMENTATION (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q4. What are types of segmentation?

👉 
1. Geographic  
2. Demographic  
3. Psychographic  
4. Behavioral  

---

### ❓ Q5. What is geographic segmentation?
👉 Based on location (city, region, country)

---

### ❓ Q6. What is demographic segmentation?
👉 Based on:
- age  
- income  
- gender  
- education  

---

### ❓ Q7. What is psychographic segmentation? (VERY IMPORTANT)

👉 Based on:
- personality  
- lifestyle  
- values  
- attitudes  

---

### ❓ Q8. Statement:
👉 Psychographics refers to personality and emotions  
👉 ✅ True  

---

### ❓ Q9. What is behavioral segmentation?
👉 Based on:
- usage  
- loyalty  
- benefits sought  

---

### ❓ Q10. Is region-based segmentation demographic?
👉 ❌ No  
👉 It is geographic  

---

# 🔴 PART 3: TARGETING (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q11. What is targeting?

---

### ❓ Q12. Criteria for target market?

👉

2. Profitable  
3. Different needs  
4. Accessible

👉 ✅ All are correct

### ❓ Q13. Why targeting is important?
👉 Focus resources on right customers
