# 🧠 PART 1: PRICING BASICS

---

### ❓ Q1. What is pricing in marketing?
👉 The process of setting the value of a product/service.

---

### ❓ Q2. What are pricing objectives?

👉 
- Survival  
- Maximum profit  
- Market share  
- Product quality leadership  

✅ Answer: All of the above  

---

### ❓ Q3. When is "Survival" pricing used?
👉 
- Intense competition  
- Overcapacity  
- Market pressure  

---

# 🔴 PART 2: MARKET SKIMMING (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q4. What is market skimming?
👉 Charging a **high initial price** and then gradually lowering it.

---

### 💡 Example:
👉 iPhone launch pricing  

---

### ❓ Q5. When does skimming work best?
👉 
- High quality product  
- High perceived value  

👉 Correct: **High price → High quality**

---

### ❓ Q6. Does skimming attract competitors?
👉 ❌ No (important correction)

---

### ❓ Q7. Why use skimming?
👉 
- Capture high-paying customers first  
- Maximize early profits  

---

# 🔴 PART 3: VALUE PRICING

---

### ❓ Q8. What is value pricing?
👉 High quality product at relatively low price  

---

### ❓ Q9. Goal of value pricing?
👉 Build loyal customers  

---

# 🔴 PART 4: PRICE DISCRIMINATION (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

### ❓ Q10. What is price discrimination?
👉 Selling same product at different prices  

---

### ❓ Q11. Types of price discrimination?

👉 
1. First degree → different price per customer  
2. Second degree → based on quantity  
3. Third degree → different groups  

---

### ❓ Q12. Second degree discrimination means?
👉 Lower price for bulk purchase  

---

# 🔴 PART 5: DYNAMIC PRICING (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

### ❓ Q13. What is dynamic pricing?
👉 Prices change based on demand  

---

### 💡 Examples:
👉 Uber surge pricing  
👉 Flight tickets  

---

### ❓ Q14. Why use dynamic pricing?
👉 Maximize revenue  

---

# 🔴 PART 6: BUNDLING

---

### ❓ Q15. What is bundling?

---

### ❓ Q16. Why bundling is used?
👉 Increase sales  

---

### ❓ Q17. Example of bundling?
👉 Combo offers  

---

### ❓ Q18. Is bundling same as price discrimination?
👉 ❌ No  

---

# 🔴 PART 7: ELASTICITY OF DEMAND (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q19. What is price elasticity of demand?
👉 Measures responsiveness of demand to price change  

---

### ❓ Q20. If elasticity is high?
👉 Demand changes significantly  

---

### ❓ Q21. If elasticity is low?
👉 Demand changes slightly  

---

### ❓ Q22. What happens if elasticity = 1 (unit elastic)?
👉 Revenue does not change  

---

### ❓ Q23. Formula concept:
👉 % change in demand / % change in price  

---
