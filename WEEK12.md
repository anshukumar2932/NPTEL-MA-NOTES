## 🧠 PART 1: SOCIAL NETWORK BASICS

---

### ❓ Q1. What is Social Network Analysis (SNA)?

👉 **Answer:**
Study of relationships (connections) between entities (people, organizations, etc.)

---

### ❓ Q2. What are nodes and ties?

👉 **Answer:**

* Nodes = individuals/entities
* Ties = relationships between them

---

### ❓ Q3. Are ties same as nodes?

👉 **Answer:** ❌ No
Nodes = people
Ties = relationships

---

### ❓ Q4. What is a dyad?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Relationship between **two individuals**

---

### ❓ Q5. What is betweenness centrality?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Measures how often a node acts as a **bridge between other nodes**

---

### ❓ Q6. Why is SNA useful in marketing?

👉 **Answer:**

* Identify influencers
* Understand information spread
* Improve targeting

---

### ❓ Q7. Can SNA be used to measure product utility?

👉 **Answer:** ❌ No
👉 That is done using **conjoint analysis**

---

### ❓ Q11. What is isolated node?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Node with very few or no connections

---

### ❓ Q12. What is network density?

👉 Measure of how connected the network is

# 🔴 PART 3: REAL APPLICATION (BULLWHIP EFFECT)

---

### ❓ Q13. What is bullwhip effect?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Small demand change → large fluctuation in supply chain

📌 From lecture: small error becomes large upstream 

---

### ❓ Q14. What causes bullwhip effect?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Lack of communication / information sharing

---

### ❓ Q15. How to reduce bullwhip effect?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Better **data sharing and coordination**

---

### ❓ Q16. What role does network play here?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Strong connections → better decisions → less error
