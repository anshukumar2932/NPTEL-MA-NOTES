# 🔴 PART 2: MARKETING MIX MODELLING (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q4. What is Marketing Mix Modelling (MMM)?
👉 Statistical analysis of sales data to measure impact of marketing activities

---

### ❓ Q5. MMM is used for?
👉 
- Measuring impact of ads  
- Forecasting sales  
- Budget allocation  

---

### ❓ Q6. Key benefit of MMM?

👉 
1. Better budget allocation  
2. Better campaign execution  
3. Scenario testing  

✅ Answer: All of the above  

---

### ❓ Q7. What insights MMM provides?

👉 
- Contribution of each activity  
- ROI of campaigns  
- Effectiveness  
- Optimal spending  

---

### ❓ Q8. MMM is:
👉 Data-driven decision tool  

---

# 🔴 PART 3: ELASTICITY (IMPORTANT CONCEPT)

---

### ❓ Q9. What is elasticity?
👉 Measure of responsiveness of demand to price change  

---

### ❓ Q10. Elasticity tells?
👉 How sensitive customers are to price  

---

### ❓ Q11. If price increases and demand falls a lot?
👉 Elastic demand  

---

### ❓ Q12. If demand doesn’t change much?
👉 Inelastic demand  

---

### ❓ Q13. Is elasticity same as optimization?
👉 ❌ No  

👉 (Common confusion)

---

# 🔴 PART 4: OPTIMIZATION

---

### ❓ Q14. What is optimization?
👉 Finding best solution under constraints  

---

### ❓ Q15. Does optimization measure substitution behavior?
👉 ❌ False  

---

### ❓ Q16. What is goal of optimization?
👉 Maximize profit / minimize cost  

---

### ❓ Q17. In R, optim() does?
👉 Minimization (default)  

---

### ❓ Q18. Does optim() maximize by default?
👉 ❌ False  

---

# 🔴 PART 5: HALO EFFECT (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

### ❓ Q19. What is Halo Effect?
👉 Positive impression in one area influences others  

---

### 💡 Example:
👉 Good brand → assume high quality  

---

### ❓ Q20. Why important in marketing?
👉 Affects customer perception
