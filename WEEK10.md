# 🧠 PART 1: TEXT MINING BASICS

---

### ❓ Q1. What is text mining?

👉 Converting **unstructured text into structured data** for analysis.

---

### ❓ Q2. What is unstructured data?

👉 Data without predefined format (text, images, videos)

---

### ❓ Q3. What is structured data?

👉 Organized data (tables, rows, columns)

---

# 🔴 PART 2: TEXT PREPROCESSING

---

### ❓ Q6. What is preprocessing?

👉 Cleaning text before analysis

---

### ❓ Q7. Steps in preprocessing?

👉 Answer:

1. Remove stopwords
2. Remove punctuation
3. Tokenization
4. Stemming

---

### ❓ Q8. What is tokenization?

👉 Breaking text into words

---

### ❓ Q9. What are stopwords?

👉 Common words (the, is, and)

---

### ❓ Q10. What is stemming?

👉 Converting words to root form

---

### ❓ Q11. Why preprocessing is important?

👉 Removes noise → improves model accuracy

---

# 🔴 PART 3: TEXT REPRESENTATION

---

### ❓ Q12. What is document-term matrix?

👉 Table:

* rows → documents
* columns → words

---

### ❓ Q13. What is TF (Term Frequency)?

👉 Frequency of word in a document

---

### ❓ Q14. What is IDF?

👉 Measures rarity of word

---

### ❓ Q15. What is TF-IDF?

👉 Importance of word = TF × IDF

---

### ❓ Q16. Why TF-IDF is better than frequency?

👉 Removes common words influence
