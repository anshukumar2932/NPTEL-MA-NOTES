# 🔴 PART 2: TEXT PREPROCESSING

---

### ❓ Q6. What is preprocessing in text mining?

👉 **Answer:**
Cleaning text before analysis

---

### ❓ Q7. Steps in preprocessing?

👉 **Answer:**

1. Remove stopwords
2. Remove punctuation
3. Tokenization
4. Stemming (optional)

---

### ❓ Q8. What is tokenization?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Breaking text into words

---

### ❓ Q9. What are stopwords?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Common words (e.g., “the”, “is”) that add no meaning

---

### ❓ Q10. What is stemming?

👉 **Answer:**
👉 Reducing words to root form
(e.g., running → run)

### ❓ What is p-value?

👉 Probability of observing result under null hypothesis

---

### ❓ Interpretation:

| ------- | ----------- |
