# 🧠 PART 1: CUSTOMER LIFETIME VALUE (CLV)

### ❓ Q1. What is Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)?
👉 Total profit generated from a customer over their entire relationship with the company.

---

### ❓ Q2. What does CLV measure?
👉 
- Purchase frequency  
- Probability of purchase  
- Repurchase behavior  
- Sequence of purchases  

---

### ❓ Q3. Why is CLV important?
👉 
- Helps identify valuable customers  
- Focus on retention over acquisition  
- Improves long-term profitability  

---

### ❓ Q4. CLV is related to?
👉 Customer retention & long-term value

---

# 🔴 PART 2: CUSTOMER CHURN

---

### ❓ Q5. What is customer churn?
👉 Customers leaving the company/service

---

### ❓ Q6. Why is churn analysis important?

👉 
1. Acquiring new customers is costly  
2. Shows retention issues  
3. Measures business failure in retention  

✅ Answer: All of the above

---

### ❓ Q7. What type of problem is churn prediction?
👉 Classification problem

# 🔴 PART 3: LOGISTIC REGRESSION (VERY IMPORTANT 🔥)

---

### ❓ Q8. Logistic regression is used for?
👉 Classification (Yes/No outcomes)

---

### ❓ Q9. Logistic regression uses which method?
👉 Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE)

---

### ❓ Q10. Logistic regression is:
👉 Classification algorithm (NOT regression in practical sense)

---

### ❓ Q11. Difference from linear regression?

👉 
- Linear → continuous output  
- Logistic → probability (0 to 1)  

---

---

### ❓ Q12. What is ROC curve?

---

### ❓ Q13. ROC curve shows?

### ❓ Q14. ROC is used for?
👉 Evaluating classification models
